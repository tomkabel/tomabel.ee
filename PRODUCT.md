# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary: Technical B2B buyers — CTOs, security leads, engineering directors evaluating deep-tech consulting and offensive security partnerships. They are technically sophisticated, skeptical of marketing fluff, and scanning for evidence of depth within seconds.

Secondary: Security researchers and peers who land on the site from published research (whitepapers, BotGuard VM analysis), looking for the author's other work and credibility signals.

The user's context: reading on a large screen late at night or during research, in a focused evaluation mindset. The interface meets them at their level of technical fluency.

## Product Purpose

A personal capability radar that makes Tom Kristian Abel's convergence of deep-tech talent (reverse engineering, AI infrastructure, offensive security, systems engineering) feel like a coherent superpower — not a résumé in 7 tabs.

Conversion goal: qualified B2B introductions via the contact form or PGP-encrypted email. Every element either demonstrates capability, establishes credibility, or removes friction from that conversion.

## Brand Personality

precision, intensity, authority.

Think tactical gear meets deep-tech lab. Everything communicates deliberate expertise — every pixel earned, every animation purposeful. The site is quiet until it needs to speak, and when it speaks, it says exactly what's necessary. No filler, no buzzwords, no self-promotional language.

## Anti-references

Anything vibe-coded or template-driven: mainstream portfolio sites that sell themselves as "modern" with generic gradients, stock icons, and SaaS-landing-page clichés. No cards-with-icons-and-text repeated endlessly. No "hero-metric" template (big number + small label). No glassmorphism as default. The site must feel built, not assembled.

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't list** — The radar chart demonstrates capability at a glance rather than bullet-pointing skills. Visual evidence over claims. Every section proves its point before making it.

2. **Expert confidence** — No self-promotional language, no hype, no em dashes. The work speaks. Restraint signals authority. Copy is direct and technical, not aspirational.

3. **Dark depth, not dark mode** — The OLED black (#020203) is atmospheric, not a UI default. Every element emerges from darkness with purpose. This is a room, not a theme toggle.

4. **Bilingual precision** — Estonian and English with equal fidelity. No translation artifacts, no language feels secondary. Language switch is instantaneous and complete.

5. **Radar coherence** — Everything ties back to the radar metaphor. The 6 axes are the site's information architecture. The chart is not decoration — it's navigation, it's the table of contents, it's the proof.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. prefers-reduced-motion respected throughout (Lenis disabled, animations skipped). Keyboard navigable with visible focus indicators. Skip link present. Semantic HTML structure. Tab order follows visual hierarchy.
